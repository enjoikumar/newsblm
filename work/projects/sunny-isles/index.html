Sunny Isles Beach Community School
Project Details ▼

The school was designed using a tilt-ip concrete construction, which received the Tilt-Up Concrete Association Achievement Award with Excellence in the Education Division. Located directly off of the Intracoastal Waterway, this 210,000 square foot facility for kindergarten through 8th grade was the first public school to be built in the Sunny Isles Beach Community. The designated site presented a considerable challege. A facility this size would typically be built on an eight acre site. SBLM needed to design this school on 2.1 acres.
